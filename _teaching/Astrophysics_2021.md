Topics I covered
======
* Gravity
    * N-body problem
    * Keppler's Laws
    * Binary Systems
    * 3-Body Problem
    * Lagrange points
    * Hydrostatic-equilibrium
* Special Relativity
    * Time dilation, legnth contraction
    * Relativistic momentum and energy
    * Doppler shifts, relativistic beaming
*  Atomic structure
    * Bohr model
    * Quantum mechanics
*  Radiative processes 
    * Spectral line transitions
    * Kirchhoff's Laws
    * Spectral stellar classification
* Statistical mechanics
    * Blackbody radiation
    * Boltzmann Equation
    * Saha equation
